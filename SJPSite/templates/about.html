About

A serious problem in our society is the unequal education of lower class students. Specifically, students in low income neighborhoods are massively underrepresented in STEM fields today.

There are numerous amounts of reason for this, often times schools in these areas receive less funding, computers are expensive to afford for some families, and many others. With no money, schools cannot afford up-to-date labs, computers, and technology necessary for stem teachings.

However, in recent years, colleges and the government have been pushing to reach out to these groups and provide programs and resources. One problem is that often times students in these situations are not aware of the support they can receive. The purpose of this website is to help aliviate this problem.
